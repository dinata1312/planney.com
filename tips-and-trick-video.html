Bali Travel Tips

Coming to Bali for the first time? Here are 10 things I WISH I knew before I moved to Bali!

10 things you need to know before coming to Bali:

  • Be aware of the monkeys!
  • Don't forget to take your card after withdrawing cash at ATM!
  • Use rideshare apps to get around.
  • Connect with a driver to chauffeur you around the island.
  • Avoid “Bali Belly” with charcoal tablets ➤ https://amzn.to/3dmCBma
  • DON'T drink tap water.
  • Tipping is optional in Indonesia.
  • Balinese ceremonies are a daily thing.
  • You’ve got visa options ➤ https://baliviza.com/
  • Airport VIP service is a must ➤ https://www.ticbali.net/

▸▸ Bonus Tip: Do a “floating breakfast” while you’re in Bali!
